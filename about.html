Let's Get To Know Us

Kopiology was founded during the COVID-19 pandemic when movement restrictions limited travel and there were no coffee shops in Machang. As a husband-and-wife team who love coffee, we saw an opportunity to bring quality coffee to the local community. Since the F&B industry could still operate during MCO, we started small with a coffee machine and a cake chiller, offering coffee and non-coffee beverages, cakes, and meatballs from a small kiosk. Over time, Kopiology grew into a fully air-conditioned café.
